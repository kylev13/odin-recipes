Jalapeño popper burgers combine juicy beef patties with creamy cheese, crispy bacon, and spicy jalapeños for a bold and flavorful burger.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Burger buns
  • Cream cheese
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Jalapeños
  • Bacon
  • Lettuce
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon until crispy and set aside.
  2. Mix softened cream cheese with diced jalapeños and cheddar cheese.
  3. Form ground beef into patties and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Cook the burger patties on a grill or skillet until fully cooked.
  5. Toast the burger buns lightly with butter.
  6. Spread the jalapeño cheese mixture onto the buns.
  7. Add the burger patties, bacon, and lettuce.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy.
